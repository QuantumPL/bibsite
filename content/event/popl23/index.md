There are three quantum PL and verification related papers being presented at POPL 2022:

- [Qunity: A Unified Language for Quantum and Classical Computing](../../publication/Voichick2023) by Finn Voichick, Liyi Li, Robert Rand, Michael Hicks
- [Proto-Quipper with Dynamic Lifting](../../publication/Fu2023) by Peng Fu, Kohei Kishida, Neil J. Ross, Peter Selinger
- [CoqQ: Foundational Verification of Quantum Programs](../../publication/Zhou2023) by Li Zhou, Gilles Barthe, Pierre-Yves Strub, Junyi Liu, Mingsheng Ying

All these papers including their appendices and expanded versions are available in the bibliography.
